\section{Research}
\begin{itemize}\parskip=0cm
\item  My current research involves the analysis of large collections of time series, and includes visualization, forecasting, reconciliation and modelling. Applications include electricity demand and smart-meter data, security sensors, manufacturing data and retail sales.
\item Since 2013, I have authored \textbf{12 peer-reviewed publications} and \textbf{2 book chapters} on topics related to plant biology and microbiology. My research has been cited \textbf{594} times and both my h-index and i10-index is \textbf{9.0}.
\item I currently supervise two Masters students at University of Cantebury, New Zealand.
\end{itemize}
